Creating this Website

The Plan and evolution of this Website.
The color Scheme was the first thing I picked out. I went with trending colors in webdesign and added some yellow accents.
It was important to me, to use colors that don't conflict with someone who has some kind of color blindness. So I tried a vew pallets to make sure the colors would still fit with each other as well.
In december I read about the design trend with a glass like finish. I really liked it and wanted to use it for me. I also visited some cool Websites with different animations and wanted to incorporate a moving background.

Fantasy Garment

Inspiration for the dress was the Character "Eowyn" from the Lord of the Rings.
Dress and the Corset are made and designed by me.
The fabric is originally used by painters, but had the perfect structure after washing it 90° Celsius. The corset is made with hard Leather from the tannery in Steffisburg and sawn with kangaroo leather. Kangaroo leather is very sturdy and can hold a lot of tension.
My sister had to model for me, so I could also take the pictures.
I think it is awesome how a dress can change the look of the whole person. At the bottom you can find a slider to compare the aesthetic.
